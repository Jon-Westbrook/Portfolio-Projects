Share ideas and connect with retail real estate professionals  in the Los Angeles area.

Held annually at the beautiful Hyatt Los Angeles Convention Center, this event is open to members and non-members alike. This year’s sessions will focus on relevant local topics, including mall redevelopment, mixed-use spaces, government relations and the current state of the hispanic market in California.

About this event series

The one-stop for education, networking and deal making. These one- to two-day regional meetings provide an opportunity for attendees to gain information about current industry issues, meet and interact with colleagues doing business in the same region and make deals. Benefits include:

  • Ballroom buffet lunch and breakfast

  • Kenote speaker meet and greet
