What drives us

Building communities

Retail centers offer more than money — a place to hang out after work, walk around with a child or experience life offline. They enrich the social fabric of the places the world calls home.

Fueling economies

Retail centers are essential to economic development, largely because of the property and sales taxes they generate. They also create jobs, buoy GDPs and fuel revenue everywhere they serve.

Inspiring innovation

Retail real estate will shape the social practices, business policies and technologies of the future. We stay on the cutting edge and report what we find, helping members step into the future with efficiency.

Our Heritage

With a $500 payment and a handshake, seven enterprising professionals established ICSC in a downtown Chicago hotel. It was 1957. The next 60 years saw those humble roots grow to more than 70,000 members and extend into 100 different countries.
